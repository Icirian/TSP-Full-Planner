Read Me

You may input any intergers in the higher tier resource boxes, and the calculator will return equivalent result. example:
input:
1 Basic Material
output:
3 Steel Ingots| 1 Titanium Ingots|
15 Iron| 3 Carbon| 5 Titanium| 5 Silicate

This means, you will need everything on the bottom row to make 1 Basic Material.
BUT, if you already have 3 Steel Ingots, you may ignore the amount of base resource needed for those 3 ingots.
Therefore you will only need 5 Silicate AND 5 titanium ore OR 1 Titanium ingots

Credit ⇆ Gold conversion
1st of all, as long as the direction of the conversion doesn't change, you do not need to reset between calculation.
Now, gold or ingots that are converted to credits are rounded down. And credit converted to gold or ingots are rounded up.
This should ensure the credits you may get are under estimated.
HOWEVER, the coefficient between ingot and credit are taken at the highest market value (142 credit/ingot). If you decide to sell 10 ingots at a time. This calculation would not be accurate at all!!!
